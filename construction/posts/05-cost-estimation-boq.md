# 05. Cost Estimation & BOQ Management

*Construction Professional Journey | June 2026*

## The Story

I prepared a BOQ (Bill of Quantities) for a project. Took two weeks.

Contractor submitted their bid: 40% lower than my estimate.

I thought they were insane. Surely they'd lose money.

Project started. By month 2, they were claiming variations.

By month 6, they'd claimed so many extras that my original BOQ didn't matter.

That's when I learned: **A bad BOQ invites claims.**

## What BOQ Is

BOQ = a list of every item needed, with:
- Quantity
- Unit (cubic meter, square meter, number)
- Description
- Unit rate (price per unit)
- Total (quantity × unit rate)

**Why it matters:**
- It's the contract price
- It's the basis for progress payments
- It's the baseline for variation claims
- It defines what's included vs. excluded

**Bad BOQ = disputes. Good BOQ = smooth project.**

## How to Build a Strong BOQ

**Step 1: Break down every item**
- Don't say "concrete works: RM 100,000"
- Say "Foundation concrete (C30, 400 m³): RM 250/m³ = RM 100,000"
- Each line must be specific

**Step 2: Research market rates**
- Get 3 quotes for every item
- Use average, not lowest
- Document your sources
- Update annually for inflation

**Step 3: Include contingencies carefully**
- Contingency: 5-10% of total
- But list what contingency covers
- Avoid "contingency for unknown" (too vague)

**Step 4: Separate facility charges**
- Labor mobilization
- Site office rental
- Insurance
- Supervision

These change monthly. BOQ quantity stays fixed. Cost per month varies.

## Real Example: Why Detail Matters

**Bad BOQ line:**
"General site work: RM 200,000"

**Contractor claims:** "We need extra site work for debris removal. That wasn't in the scope."

You: "It was in 'general site work!'"

Contractor: "No, that's unreasonable interpretation."

You lose the argument because your BOQ is unclear.

---

**Good BOQ line:**
"Site clearance and debris removal (including excavation, crushing, disposal): RM 150/m³ × 500m³ = RM 75,000"

**If contractor claims extra:** "Debris removal is already in the BOQ at RM 150/m³. If you're claiming more, show me why the additional volume exists."

You win because your BOQ is specific.

## What You Need to Know

**A BOQ is your protection.**

Spend time on it. Get it right. Contractors won't find loopholes.

Bad BOQ = expensive disputes. Good BOQ = smooth project.
