# 10. Data Analytics & Reporting

*Construction Professional Journey | June 2026*

## The Story

I prepared a monthly report: 20 pages of tables and charts. Numbers everywhere.

Client glanced at it. "So... are we on track or not?"

I realized: **I was drowning them in data. They wanted insights.**

That's when I learned Power BI. It changed everything.

## What Power BI Does

Power BI = data visualization software

Instead of 20 pages of tables, you get:

**One dashboard showing:**
- Green/red lights (on track or not)
- Trends (are we getting better or worse)
- Where problems are (which activity is behind)
- What to do (here's the recommendation)

## Real Example: Before vs. After

**Before Power BI (old way):**

*Monthly Report Page 5:*

| Activity | Budget | Spent | Schedule | Cost | Trend |
|----------|--------|-------|----------|------|-------|
| Foundation | RM 5M | RM 5.2M | 103% | 104% | ↑ |
| Structure | RM 20M | RM 8.5M | 45% | 43% | ↑ |
| MEP | RM 15M | RM 1.2M | 10% | 8% | ↓ |

*Client reads it.* "Uh... which one am I supposed to worry about?"

**After Power BI (new way):**

**Dashboard:**
- Red light on Foundation (over budget, over schedule)
- Green light on Structure (slightly ahead)
- Green light on MEP (behind schedule but under budget)

**Insight:** "Foundation contractor is struggling. Recommend: accelerate other activities to absorb foundation delay. Or bring in support crew."

Client: "Got it. Let's do that."

## The Power BI Workflow

**Step 1: Connect data sources**
- Link to CostX (cost data)
- Link to P6 (schedule data)
- Link to site reports (progress data)

**Step 2: Create visualizations**
- Dashboard showing traffic lights (red/yellow/green)
- Charts showing trends
- Maps showing where delays are
- Alerts showing what needs attention

**Step 3: Refresh monthly**
- Update all data
- Dashboards auto-update
- Send to client

**Step 4: Present insights**
- Don't just show data
- Say: "Here's what's happening. Here's what to do about it."

## Real Example: Spotting a Problem

**Month 4 Power BI dashboard shows:**
- Labor cost trending up
- But productivity is down
- Forecast shows RM 2M overrun

**Your analysis:** "Labor efficiency has dropped from 85% to 70%. At this rate, we'll overrun by RM 2M. Recommendation: audit work methods, provide training, or consider productivity bonus to improve focus."

**Client sees this in dashboard. Takes action. Problem solved.**

Without Power BI, you'd have 30 pages of data and no one would read it.

## What You Need to Know

**Data is only useful if it's understood.**

Power BI transforms data into understanding.

Learn it. Use it. Your clients will trust you more.
