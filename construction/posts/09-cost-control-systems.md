# 09. Cost Control Systems

*Construction Professional Journey | June 2026*

## The Story

I managed a RM 100M project. Finished RM 5M over budget.

I thought I was tracking costs. But I wasn't really.

When I analyzed what went wrong:
- Material costs: +RM 3M (market inflation, my fault for not locking in prices early)
- Labor costs: +RM 1.5M (slower progress, my fault for not managing schedule)
- Contingency: -RM 0.5M (we didn't need all contingency, my win)

Net overrun: RM 4M (one item was my fault, two items weren't my fault, so partial credit)

I learned: **Without cost control systems, you don't know where your money is going.**

## What RIB CostX Does

CostX = cost management software

It tracks:
- Actual spend vs. budget
- By category (materials, labor, equipment)
- By phase (foundation, superstructure, finishing)
- By contractor (who's spending what)
- Forecasted final cost (will you go over?)

## The Monthly Review

**Every month, you run a cost report:**

| Item | Budget | Spent | Forecast | Variance |
|------|--------|-------|----------|----------|
| Concrete | RM 10M | RM 4M | RM 12M | -RM 2M (over) |
| Steel | RM 8M | RM 2M | RM 8M | ±0 (on track) |
| Labor | RM 15M | RM 7M | RM 16M | -RM 1M (over) |
| **Total** | **RM 100M** | **RM 40M** | **RM 105M** | **-RM 5M (over)** |

**Action:**
- Concrete over? Investigate why. Can you control it?
- Steel on track? Good.
- Labor over? Is it schedule delay? Higher wages? Inefficiency?
- Forecast: if you do nothing, you'll finish RM 5M over. What will you cut?

## Real Example: Catching Problems Early

**Month 3:** Concrete forecast is already RM 2M over
- You investigate
- Find: supplier prices jumped 20%
- You have 3 options:
  - Accept the cost
  - Find cheaper supplier (lose time)
  - Reduce quantity (change design)
- You decide: reduce some non-critical concrete. Save RM 1M

**Month 6:** Labor forecast is RM 1M over
- You investigate
- Find: worker productivity is 70% of estimate
- You have 2 options:
  - Accept the slower pace (project runs longer)
  - Hire more workers (costs money upfront, saves time)
- You decide: hire more workers, finish on time

**Result:** Early warnings let you make good decisions.

Without CostX? You wouldn't know until project end. Too late to fix.

## What You Need to Know

**Cost control isn't about preventing overspend. It's about detecting it early.**

Early detection = early action = smaller damage.

Use CostX. Update monthly. Review religiously.
