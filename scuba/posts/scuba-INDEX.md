## The Philosophy Behind These Posts

Each post starts with a real story because **diving isn't learned from textbooks.** It's learned from experience, near-misses, and moments that change how you see the ocean.

My job as an instructor is to help you learn from other people's experiences—so you don't have to learn every lesson the hard way.
